Craft Beer Styles

India Pale Ales

An extension of the pale ale variety, with higher gravity ranges and enhanced hop presence. One of the most popular craft beer styles in the world.

Pale Ales

Pale ales have a full flavour whilst not being too heavy. Even though the beer originated in England, years of experimentation have made the pale ale a cornerstone of the Australian craft beer scene as well.

Sours / Wild Beers

Sour / Wild Beers is a category encompassing a myriad of non-traditional sour ales which are typically brewed with an ale yeast and then inoculated with souring bacteria and yeasts typically varieties such as; Lactobacillus, Brettanomyces Pediococcus, and Acetobacter.

Lagers

Lager is the most commonly availble beer style in the world. The bottom-fermented techniques used to brew this beer result in a crisp, refreshing beer, though Lagers are a very diverse style! They can range all the way from pale, amber and dark. It encompasses Bavarian styles too, Pilsners, Bocks, Dunkels and Oktoberfest. Happens to be a favourite of my Web Design Tutor as well!
