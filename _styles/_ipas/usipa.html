American IPA

Floral, fruity, citrus-like, piney or resinous American-variety hop character, the IPA beer style is all about hop flavour, aroma and bitterness.

Characteristics

  • 6-12 SRM
  • 50-70 IBU
  • 6.3-7.5% ABV
