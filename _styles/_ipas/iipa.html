Imperial India Pale Ale

American craft beer lovers are huge fans of the IPA. The quest for more of the India Pale Ale flavour has led them down the path to the Imoerial India Pale Ale, a stronger version of the American IPa, which boasts even more hoppy flavour, aroma and bitterness. Imperial India Pale Ale is darker in colour than the American IPA, substantially more bitterm and high in alcohol by volume. This all-American take on the IPA leaves craft beer fans with plenty of new creations to try. The Imperial India Pale Ale features high hop bitterness, flavour and aroma. Hop character is fresh and evident from the utilisation of any variety of hops. Alcohol content is medium-high to high and notably evident with a medium-high to full body. This tyle intends to exhibit the fresh, evident character of hops.

Characteristics

  • 2-9 SRM
  • 65-100 IBU
  • 7.6-10.6% ABV
