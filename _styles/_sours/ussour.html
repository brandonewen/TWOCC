Sour / Wild Beers

American Sour

The aciditty present in sour beer is usually in the form of lactic, acetic and other organic acids naturally developed with acidified malt in the mash, or produced during fermentation by the use of various microorganisms. These beers may derive their sour flavour from pure cultured forms of souring agents or from the influence of barrel ageing.
