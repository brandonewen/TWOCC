Pale Ales

English-Style Pale Ale (ESB)

"Extra Special Bitter." This style is known for its balance and the interplay between malt and hop bitterness. English pale ales display earthy, herbal English-variety hop character. Medium to high hop bitterness, flavour and aroma should be evident. They yeast strains used in these beers lend a fruitness to their aromatics and flavour, referred to as esters. The residual malt and defining sweetness of this richly flavoured and medium bodied.

Characteristics

  • 5-16 SRM
  • 20-40 IBU
  • 4.5-5.5% ABV
