Pale Ales

English-Style Bitter

The English-Style Bitter is a very sessionable, lower-alcohol, malt-driven style. Broad style description commonly associated with cask-conditioned beers. The light to medium-bodied ordinary bitter is gold to copper in clour, with a low residual malt sweetness. Hop bitterness is medium.

Characteristics

  • 5-12 SRM
  • 20-35 IBU
  • 3-4.2% ABV
